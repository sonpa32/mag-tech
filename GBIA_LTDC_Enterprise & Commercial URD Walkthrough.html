📋 URD Structure: Every Sub-Spec Follows a Consistent Template

1. Introduction & Scope

Purpose, operational context, design principles, system relationships, capability evolution profile

2. Requirements

Functional, non-functional (performance, security, backup), hardware, integration, standards compliance

3. Delivery

Deployment model, testing (FAT/SAT/SIT/UAT), documentation, training, warranty & support

4. Appendices

Glossary, data ownership, interface catalogue, availability model, compliance boundary, risk assessment

Key Design Principle: Every sub-specification mandates Vietnam data residency (Decree 53/2022), IEC 62443 security classification, integration via ESB/API Gateway, PDPL 91/2025 privacy compliance, and a capability evolution roadmap from Day 1 through to Ultimate Capacity at 50 MPPA.

Customer & Partner Platforms | 5 Systems

The relationship layer: 360-degree passenger profiles, loyalty currency, commercial partner management, and the merchant self-service ecosystem.

👥 Customer & Partner Management

5 sub-specs · 510 requirements
👤
CRM Platform (ICT-0301)

121 requirements
Airport-owned system managing customer profiles, campaign orchestration, SKU-level transaction analytics, consent-based personalisation across all AeroCity touchpoints.

"Calm, not aggressive" engagement: AI determines optimal timing/channel, max 3 commercial offers per hour per passenger. Target: 40%+ app-registered passengers with enriched profiles within 12 months.

🎁
Loyalty Programme (ICT-0336)

145 requirements
Airport-owned loyalty currency: points earn/burn across all commercial touchpoints, tier management, partner settlement, airport service entitlements, reward fulfilment.

Operates as a revenue stream: participating parties pay in when points are issued, cash out on redemption. Immutable transaction ledger. Real-time earn within 60s of POS transaction. Target: 40%+ enrolled within 12 months.

💾
Merchant Portal (ICT-0302)

152 reqs
Tenant self-service: sales reporting, lease compliance, operational notifications, marketing submission, staff pass requests. Reduces GBIA admin overhead.

🤝
Partner Lifecycle (ICT-0303)

82 reqs
End-to-end partner onboarding, contract management, performance tracking, compliance monitoring across all commercial partners.

🎫
Digital Voucher Engine (ICT-0305)

110 reqs
Generates, distributes, redeems digital vouchers. Disruption compensation, promotional campaigns, loyalty rewards. Anti-fraud and single-use enforcement.

Integration Pattern: CRM + Loyalty + Voucher Engine form a tightly coupled trio. CRM provides customer context, Loyalty provides earn/burn rules, Voucher Engine provides the fulfilment mechanism. All exchange data via the Commercial Event Streaming platform (ICT-0322). CRM and Loyalty are companion systems with bidirectional interfaces but independent domain ownership.

Enterprise Domain

Identity & Access Control | 5 Systems

Zero-trust identity and access management for staff, passengers, and partners. Biometric-first, app-delivered credentials with ICAO Annex 17 and PDPL 91/2025 compliance.

🔐 Identity & Access Management

5 sub-specs · 597 requirements · SL-3 Critical
🔒
Physical Access Control (ICT-0277)

99 reqs · SL-3 · 99.99% availability

2,500+ controlled access points (doors, turnstiles, mantraps). NFC via Staff App as primary credential, facial recognition as biometric fallback (ISO 30107-3 PAD), contactless cards for visitors. Anti-passback enforcement across all zones.

Flight-driven access policies via AODB integration: ground handlers access gate zones only during allocated turnaround windows. Fail-safe/fail-secure by zone per ICAO Annex 17.

👤
Physical Identity Mgmt (ICT-0278)

131 reqs

Lifecycle management of all physical access rights: issuance, modification, suspension, revocation. Attribute-Based Access Control (ABAC) combining role + time + location + training status + behavioural context.

Training-driven access activation: privileges progressively unlock as certifications are completed in LMS. Auto-suspension on expiry.

💻
Digital IAM (ICT-0279)

150 reqs
SSO, MFA, role-based access for all enterprise applications. Privileged Access Management (PAM). Joiner/mover/leaver automation linked to HR lifecycle.

🔑
Customer IAM (ICT-0337)

71 reqs · 99.99%
B2C identity: passenger SSO, social login, passwordless auth, granular consent per PDPL. Separate from enterprise DIAM. 50M+ accounts.

🔐
Consent & Privacy (ICT-0327)

146 reqs
PDPL 91/2025 compliance engine. Consent verification API in the critical path of biometric processing. Default deny if consent cannot be verified. 5M+ active consent records. 200K-500K verifications/day.

🛡 Zero-Trust Security Model

Biometric-First

Staff App NFC as primary credential; facial recognition (ISO 30107-3 PAD) as fallback; cards only for visitors/contingency

Training-Gated Access

Access privileges unlock progressively as staff complete LMS certifications; auto-suspend on expiry

AODB-Linked Policies

Dynamic access tied to flight schedules: ground handlers access gate zones only during allocated turnaround windows

Consent Before Processing

No biometric data processing without verified consent. Default deny. Consent & Privacy platform in critical path

Integration Backbone | 4 Systems

The nervous system connecting all 61 enterprise systems. Point-to-point integrations are prohibited; all data exchange must traverse this centralised integration layer.

🔄 Integration & Middleware

4 sub-specs · 424 requirements
📨
Enterprise Service Bus (ICT-0320)

131 reqs · SL-3 · 99.99%

Message-oriented middleware: pub/sub, request-reply, guaranteed delivery, content-based routing, protocol mediation (AMQP, REST, SOAP, JMS, MQTT, OPC UA via IT/OT Bridge). Contract-first integration.

50K-500K+ messages/hour at peak. 40+ connected systems Day 1. Separate from OT ESB (ICT-0220).

🔧
API Gateway (ICT-0321)

123 reqs · 99.99%

Single point of entry for all programmatic access. OAuth 2.0/OIDC authentication (via DIAM), rate limiting, API versioning, developer portal, usage analytics. Direct backend access prohibited.

10K-1M+ API calls/hour. Consumers: airlines, handlers, Passenger App, Staff App, third-party developers.

⚡
Commercial Event Streaming (ICT-0322)

98 reqs
Enterprise event bus (Kafka-based) for real-time business events: transactions, loyalty events, CRM triggers, offer activations, booking confirmations. Decouples producers from consumers.

☁
iPaaS (ICT-0323)

72 reqs
Cloud-based integration for SaaS-to-SaaS and SaaS-to-on-prem workflows. Pre-built connectors (Salesforce, SAP, Workday). Low-code workflow orchestration. Cloud API federation via API Gateway.

Integration Architecture: Four Pillars

Aviation Domain

AODB, A-CDM, BHS, CUPPS → via OT ESB & IT/OT Bridge

ESB + API Gateway + Event Streaming + iPaaS

Point-to-point prohibited. All 149 systems integrate via these four pillars

OT Domain

BMS, VMS, PACS, FAS → via OT ESB & data diodes

Commercial Systems

CRM, Loyalty, POS, Billing → Event Streaming + ESB

External Partners

Airlines, ride-hailing, GDS → API Gateway + iPaaS

Data Platform

Lakehouse, BI, ML → Event Streaming + batch ETL

Enterprise Domain

Cybersecurity | 4 Systems

Protecting 25,000+ endpoints across IT and OT. IEC 62443 zone model, converged SOC, zero-trust endpoint protection, and centralised secrets vault.

🛡 Cybersecurity & Threat Defence

4 sub-specs · 243 requirements
👁
Enterprise SOC (ICT-0325)

95 reqs · SL-3 · 99.99% · 24/7/365

SIEM for log aggregation and correlation (50K+ EPS sustained, 150K+ EPS peak). SOAR for playbook-driven incident response. Threat Intelligence Platform (TIP). Single pane of glass across all IEC 62443 zones (Level 0-5).

Staffed: Tier 1/2/3 analysts, SOC manager, CISO. 25K+ monitored endpoints. 12-month hot/warm, 24-month total log retention. NIST CSF 2.0 aligned.

💥
Network Security (ICT-0324)

15 reqs · SL-3

Next-gen firewalls, IDS/IPS, network segmentation, DDoS protection. Implements IEC 62443 zone/conduit model with hardware data diodes for unidirectional OT-to-IT data flow. Micro-segmentation at application level. TLS 1.3 minimum.

🔎
Extended Detection & Response (ICT-0326)

55 reqs
Endpoint protection, advanced threat hunting, behavioural analysis, automated containment. Covers servers, workstations, mobile, containers. Feeds SOC SIEM for correlated detection.

🔐
Secrets Management (ICT-0338)

78 reqs · 99.99%
HSM-backed vault (FIPS 140-2 Level 3). Static secrets with enforced rotation, dynamic short-lived credentials, PKI/X.509 lifecycle, SSH keys, encryption-as-a-service. Zero standing credentials. 10K-50K+ secrets. Namespace multi-tenancy.

🛡 Defence-in-Depth: Enterprise Security Stack

Prevention

NSS firewalls & micro-segmentation → XDR endpoint protection → Secrets Management (zero standing secrets) → IAM access control

Detection

SOC SIEM correlation (50K+ EPS) → XDR behavioural analysis → Network anomaly detection → OT security monitoring

Response

SOAR automated playbooks → Tiered analyst escalation (T1/T2/T3) → Automated containment → Forensic evidence preservation

Compliance

Vietnamese Cybersecurity Law → IEC 62443 → Decree 53/2022 → 24-month log retention → NIST CSF 2.0

Key Takeaways & Next Steps

The Enterprise Division URD delivers a comprehensive, integrated specification for every non-aviation and non-OT system at GBIA.

🎯
Commercial Differentiation

29 commercial + digital systems form a complete digital marketplace: from aeronautical billing through loyalty-driven omnichannel retail to AI-powered passenger engagement.

The airport operates as a platform business. GBIA is facilitator, not merchant of record. "Calm, not aggressive" engagement.

🧠
Data-Driven Operations

7 data & AI systems transform raw data into predictive intelligence. Lakehouse architecture with contract-first integration provides a single copy of truth.

165 data governance requirements, the highest single sub-spec count, reflecting the criticality of data quality at scale. Vietnam AI Law 134/2025 conformity.

🛡
Security by Design

9 identity + security systems implement defence-in-depth from physical access to cyber threat detection. Zero-trust, biometric-first, training-gated access model.

Vietnamese regulatory compliance embedded in every sub-spec: PDPL 91/2025, Cybersecurity Law, Decree 53/2022, AI Law 134/2025.

📋 Cross-Cutting Design Principles

Vietnam Data Residency

All personal and operational data stored in-country. Enforced at lakehouse, cloud, and application levels per Decree 53/2022

IEC 62443 Classification

Every system assigned a security level (SL-1 to SL-3). Zone-based segmentation. Hardware data diodes for OT boundaries

No Point-to-Point

All systems integrate via ESB, API Gateway, Event Streaming, or iPaaS. Contract-first integration with published data contracts

Capability Evolution

Every sub-spec includes a roadmap from Day 1 baseline through Phase 1, Phase 2, and Ultimate Capacity at 50 MPPA

Direct Relationships

Staff: direct via Staff App (90%+ adoption, 100% digital). Passengers: direct via GBIA App (50%+ adoption)
